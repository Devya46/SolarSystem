Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between FPC and ETC?
FPC uses flat plates and works efficiently in colder climates. ETC uses glass tubes and is more economical for warmer regions.
Does it work on cloudy days?
Yes, both systems work in diffused sunlight. For continuous backup, an electric element is provided.
What is the warranty?
5 years on complete system, 15 years on tank, 25 years on collectors.
