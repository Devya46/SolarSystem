Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average cost of a residential solar system?
Cost ranges from ₹40,000 to ₹60,000 per kW depending on components. After subsidy, it's even lower.
How much roof space is needed?
Typically 100 sq. ft. for 1 kW system. We'll assess your roof during free survey.
Do I need permission from the electricity board?
We handle all documentation and net metering approval on your behalf.
What is the warranty period?
Solar panels: 25 years performance warranty. Inverter: 5-10 years. Workmanship: 5 years.
